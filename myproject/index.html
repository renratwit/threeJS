Full-Stack Development Co-op

Diamond Diagnostics

After taking a web development course that covered HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and SQL, I joined Diamond Diagnostics as a full-stack develper co-op. I was introduced to frameworks (Angular and AngularJS) and NodeJS, Express, and MongoDB. During my time at Diamond Diagnostics, I contributed to the company's internal business application that facilitated the daily functions of every employee.

My interest for web development continued after my co-op at Diamond Diagnostcs. I picked up ReactJS using free resources online and built MERN stack projects from the ground up.

Osteoarthritis Research Co-op

WIT Digital Health Lab

In my senior year, I was introduced to the opportunity of joining Dr. Ming Zhang's research group after taking his Image Processing course the previous semester. Dr. Zhang focuses on Biomedical Image Processing and Computer Vision with machine learning. I took this opportunity to explore a different avenue my degree can offer.

My first task was to plan and design a conference poster for the group's most recent project for the American College of Rheumatology conference. I was then offered the opportunity to propose a project for me to lead. I proposed an automated process to aid diagnosis of osteoarthritis in finger joints. My proposal was accepted and I was given a grant for this project.

The project was built on image processing fundamentals using Python.
